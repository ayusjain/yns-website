## About This Episode

Before the guests, before the stories — there was a question.

Why do we only celebrate the famous, the wealthy, the already-proven? What about the person next door who built something quietly, survived something privately, or chose a different path entirely?

This is where Your Neighbourhood Stories begins.

## What This Show Is About

- The stories that don't make it to mainstream media
- Real, unfiltered conversations — no PR, no polish
- Builders, dreamers, and quiet revolutionaries from every walk of life
- A new episode every 2nd Thursday

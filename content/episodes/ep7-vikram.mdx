## About This Episode

Dr. Vikram was a dentist running his own practice for six years. Stable. Respected. Sorted. Then one Sunday night at TGIF with his best friend Ankur — a single comment cracked something open. The idea never left.

What followed was not one reinvention but a pattern: dentist, MBA, management consultant. Always moving before the market. Always one step ahead.

- Why he chose an MBA and how his family reacted

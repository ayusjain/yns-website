## About This Episode

Hemanth spent over a decade climbing the corporate ladder across companies like GE, Dell, and HP. A restructuring left him unexpectedly sidelined — and what followed was a quiet, painful re-evaluation of identity, salary, and purpose.

This episode traces the months of uncertainty, the internal conversations that followed, and the moment he chose to walk away from a life many would call secure.

## What We Talk About

- The years spent climbing corporate ranks and the restructuring that changed everything
- The silent weight of salary, masculinity, and purpose when a steady paycheck stops
- How small daily choices compounded into the courage to step away
- Rebuilding after the exit: practical steps, mental shifts, and finding new meaning

## About This Episode

In this first episode of The Experience Of, a woman recalls a night in Delhi that still has no explanation. What began as an ordinary Diwali evening became something cold, physical, and impossible to make sense of.

She remembers the room locked from inside, the body refusing to move, and the terror of being unable to call out. The story is short, but the feeling stays long after.

## What We Talk About

- One night that changed the way she understood fear
- The shock of being unable to scream or move
- The strange physicality of a presence that felt close enough to press her down
- Why some experiences remain with us long after the night is over

## Notes

This is the first episode of a new short-form format from Your Neighbourhood Stories — one single experience, one story, no extra noise.

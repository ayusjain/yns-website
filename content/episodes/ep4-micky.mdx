## About This Episode

What does it take to build something when no one has done it before you, in a city that doesn't have a roadmap, at an age when most people are still figuring out their next exam?

Micky started an international export business at 18 years old in Bhopal. No internet. No mentor. No one to call. Just the audacity to begin.

In this conversation, we talk about the quiet revolutions that happen before the world notices — the early mornings, the cold shoulders, the moments of doubt that never make it to the highlight reel.

## What We Talk About

- Starting a fashion export business at 18 with zero guidance
- Why "ignorance is bliss" can be your greatest competitive advantage
- How rejection becomes direction — "The NOs are the biggest YES in your life"
- The Indian body type problem in fashion — and why it's the blueprint's fault, not yours
- Building Swarasa during COVID and choosing people over profit
- How AI is changing the way we understand bodies and fit
- What success really means after 30 years of building
